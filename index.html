About Us

The University Placement Cell is committed to providing comprehensive placement support to our students. We work closely with a wide range of recruiters from various industries to ensure that our students have access to a variety of placement opportunities. We also offer a range of career development services to help our students prepare for the job market.

Recruiters

We are proud to partner with a large number of leading recruiters from various industries. Our recruiters recognize the high quality of our students and are eager to offer them placement opportunities.

Students

We are dedicated to providing our students with the support they need to succeed in the job market. We offer a range of career development services, including:

  • Career counseling
  • Resume and cover letter writing workshops
  • Mock interviews
  • Career fairs

We also maintain a comprehensive job database that is accessible to all students.

Contact Us

We are always happy to hear from you. Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions.
